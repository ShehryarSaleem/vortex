Common Questions

Typically, you'll need a valid passport, completed visa application form, passport-sized photographs, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, bank statements showing sufficient funds, travel insurance, and return flight tickets. Requirements may vary by destination.

Processing times vary depending on the destination and type of visa. Tourist visas typically take 2-4 weeks, while student and work visas may take longer. We recommend applying well in advance of your planned travel date.

While we cannot guarantee visa approval as decisions are made by the respective embassies or consulates, we ensure your application is complete, accurate, and properly prepared to maximize your chances of success. Our expertise significantly improves approval rates.

We provide visa services for multiple destinations including Schengen countries (Austria, France, Germany, Greece, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, Switzerland), USA, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. We can assist with tourist, student, business, and other visa types for these destinations.

Our service fees vary depending on the type of visa and destination. In addition to our service fee, you'll need to pay the embassy/consulate visa fee, which varies by country. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

If your visa is rejected, we can help you understand the reasons for rejection and guide you on how to address them. In many cases, you can reapply after addressing the issues. We'll review your application and provide recommendations for a stronger reapplication.

Travel insurance is mandatory for Schengen visa applications and highly recommended for most other destinations. It provides coverage for medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and other unforeseen circumstances. We can help you obtain appropriate travel insurance that meets visa requirements.
