What You Need to Know

Tourist visas allow you to visit a country for leisure, sightseeing, and recreational purposes. The requirements and processing times vary by destination, but typically include:

  • Valid passport with sufficient validity
  • Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
  • Financial proof demonstrating sufficient funds
  • Travel insurance (required for some destinations)
  • Return flight tickets or proof of onward travel
